Our Story

Slow-Cooked. Smashed.
Unforgettable.

Here's where we don't cut corners. Our pork is marinated in housemade mojo. Our chicken is all white meat, brined so it stays extra juicy. Our beef is built on a handcrafted seasoning blend. Then we slow-cook every meat and smash it on the grill, so you get the deep flavor of something slow-cooked with the crave-worthy texture of something griddled. Every sauce and salsa is made from scratch, right down to our green and red hot sauces.

That obsession runs four generations deep. Grandma sparked it with her black beans, though we've reworked that recipe so many times it's become entirely our own. The mojo pork is my mom's. The beef and the rice? Both inspired by my son. And we cook it all clean: no seed oils, ever, only avocado and olive oil. Every item on our menu is meticulously crafted to hit maximum flavor on the palate while keeping the textures that keep you coming back.
